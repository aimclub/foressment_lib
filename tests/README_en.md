<h1 align="center">TESTS</h1>

<p align="left">
Tests are presented in separate files, while the names of files are based on the modules that were analysed:

- ```assessor_tests.py```: assessment of the current state of complex technical objects (**Assessor**);
- ```forecaster_tests.py```: forecasting of the future state of complex technical objects (**Forecaster**);
- ```extractor_tests.py```: knowledge extraction from raw and fuzzy data (**Extractor**);
- ```preprocessor_tests.py```: preprocessing of raw and fuzzy data (**Preprocessor**);

Console output of tests is presented in ```./results``` folder.
</p>
